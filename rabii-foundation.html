Three ways we work
alongside a community.

Every programme is proposed, run and reviewed with the people it serves — that's what keeps results standing after we've moved on.

Education

Scholarships, school supplies and teacher training that keep children — especially girls — in the classroom through to graduation.

62 schools supported

Healthcare

Mobile clinics and maternal-health support that bring care within walking distance.

Livelihoods

Farming, clean water and small-business training that let families plan past next season.
