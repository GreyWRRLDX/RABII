Four ways to work with us.

Every partnership starts from what you have to offer — funding, products, people, or expertise — and what a community actually needs.

Corporate giving

Annual or multi-year funding directed to a specific programme or region, with reporting matched to your fiscal calendar.

Best for: sustained CSR budgets

Employee engagement

Volunteer placements, skills-based projects and matched-giving campaigns your staff can take part in directly.

Best for: employee experience goals

In-kind & product support

Equipment, medical supplies, agricultural inputs or logistics support delivered straight into an active programme.

Best for: goods, equipment, logistics

Strategic co-design

Multi-year commitments where your organisation helps shape a new programme from the ground up, alongside communities.

Best for: foundations, agencies

How a partnership comes together.

Most partners are live within a quarter. Larger, multi-year agreements typically take two.

01

Conversation

A call to understand what your organisation wants to achieve and where our current programmes already overlap with it.

02

Proposal

We bring back a specific programme, region and budget — never a generic pitch deck.

03

Agreement

Terms, reporting cadence and success measures are set in writing before any funds move.

04

Delivery & reporting

Programmes run on the ground with your organisation kept informed through the cadence you chose in step three.
